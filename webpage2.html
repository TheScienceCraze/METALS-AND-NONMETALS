Physical Properties of Metals

  • Malleablity

    The property of metals by which they can be beaten into thin sheets without forming a crack

  • Ductility

    The property of metals in which they can be drawn into wires

    Conductivity

    The property of metals in which they can allow heat and electricity to pass through them.
